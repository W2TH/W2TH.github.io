Punta Sur Ecological Park

Located on Cozumel’s southernmost tip, Punta Sur Eco Beach Park (Faro Celerain Ecological Reserve) spans 2,500 acres (1,011 hectares) of coastal wilderness, coral reefs, and Caribbean ocean. Here you can find ancient Maya ruins, a picturesque lighthouse, and sandy beaches—plus exotic birds, crocodiles, and sea turtles.

Things to Know Before You Go

  • Punta Sur Eco Beach Park is a must-see destination for wildlife lovers
  • Remember to bring sun protection, a swimsuit, and plenty of water
  • Be careful not to touch or disturb the wildlife, whether you’re on land or swimming on the reef

El Mirador

Located on Cozumel’s rugged eastern coast, El Mirador lookout is a rocky seascape dotted with natural bridges, tide pools, and stone spires. This wild, undeveloped area looks out onto the open Caribbean Sea and offers a breezy escape from Cozumel’s more touristy areas.

Things to Know Before You Go

  • El Mirador lookout is not a swimming beach. Waves and currents here can be dangerous
  • It’s a good idea to wear closed-toe shoes with sufficient traction for climbing on the rock formations
  • This part of Cozumel is relatively undeveloped, so there aren’t many service areas nearby. Bring food, water, and other necessities with you
  • Parking at the beach is free
Read more about El Mirador on the Viator website

Plaza del Sol

Located adjacent to Cozumel’s ferry port, Plaza del Sol is the cultural and economic heart of San Miguel. With bustling shops, restaurants, and breezy ocean views, this sunlit square is a good place to people-watch, browse souvenirs, and grab a bite. When evening nears, the street scene transforms as locals and tourists flock to nearby bars for live music and dancing.

Things to Know Before You Go

  • Plaza del Sol is a must-see for culture and nightlife lovers
  • It’s a good idea to carry cash (in Mexican pesos) to use at local restaurants, bars, and shops
  • Remember to bring sun protection and comfortable walking shoes
  • While many locals speak at least some English, it can be helpful to learn some simple Spanish phrases
