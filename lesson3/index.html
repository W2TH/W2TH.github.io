Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help people to know what activities they can do in Idaho according to its weather. In this website, they will find reliable information of Idaho's weather, a list of all activities offered by many companies, a gallery which shows how people enjoy their vacations, and all information they will need if they are planning to go out of their comfort zone and experience what Idaho offers them.
